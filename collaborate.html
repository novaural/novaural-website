Partnership Models

Select the model that best fits your organisation's needs and goals.

🏥

NHS Trusts & AHSNs

Partner with us to run a clinical validation pilot within your audiology department.

  • Co-designed pilot programme tailored to your service
  • Quantitative data on fitting efficiency and patient outcomes
  • Potential reduction in follow-up visits and waiting times
  • Report suitable for commissioners and service evaluations
  • No upfront cost during the pilot phase
Ideal for: NHS trusts looking to innovate audiology services and demonstrate measurable quality improvement.
🏢

Private Clinics

Become an early adopter and differentiate your practice with verified fitting technology.

  • Early access to ReFittingLab before commercial launch
  • Direct technical support and onboarding
  • Co-development of clinical workflows for your practice
  • Priority pricing and terms for early partners
  • Marketing support — verified outcomes you can share with patients
Ideal for: Forward-thinking audiologists who want to lead with evidence-based fitting verification.
🎓

Research Institutions

Collaborate on audiology research using our platform and methodology.

  • Access to our phonemic discrimination dataset and algorithms
  • Joint publication opportunities
  • Co-development of new language packs and test protocols
  • Student/researcher access for academic projects
  • Grant co-application support (Innovate UK, NIHR, etc.)
Ideal for: Universities and research centres working in audiology, speech sciences, or hearing aid technology.
🎧

Consumer Audio Manufacturers

Integrate speech-intelligibility technology into your consumer audio devices — earbuds, headphones, AR/VR headsets, and more.

  • SDK or API integration for on-device speech discrimination evaluation
  • Per-listener frequency profile generation for EQ personalisation
  • Patent-protected technology (47 claims) — licensing available
  • Works with any device architecture with adjustable frequency bands
  • Differentiator for product marketing: "clinically-inspired audio personalisation"
Ideal for: Earbuds, headphones, AR/VR headsets, and audio eyewear manufacturers looking for a science-backed audio personalisation feature.
