Beyond Clinical

Personalised Hearing Enhancement.
Speech Clarity Without Raising Volume.

Novaural's patent-protected technology isn't limited to hearing aids. It can be applied to any consumer audio device with adjustable frequency output — from AirPods and wireless earbuds to headphones, AR/VR headsets, and audio-enabled eyewear.

The Hidden Problem

Volume Isn't the Answer

Millions of people struggle to understand speech through their earbuds — not because they need hearing aids, but because their ears have subtle frequency gaps that a simple volume increase cannot fix.

🔊

The Volume Trap

When speech sounds unclear, the natural reaction is to turn up the volume. But this amplifies all frequencies equally — including those you hear perfectly well, exposing healthy frequencies to potentially damaging sound pressure levels over time.

🧠

Hidden Listening Effort

Even when you "can hear" through your earbuds, your brain is constantly working harder to fill in the gaps at frequencies where your perception is weaker. This unconscious effort causes listening fatigue — making calls, podcasts, and music draining over extended use.

📉

A Growing Gap

Age-related hearing changes begin as early as your 30s, typically affecting high frequencies first. Most people don't notice — they unknowingly compensate with volume, gradually damaging the frequencies that still work well.

Our Approach

Frequency-Specific Intelligence, Not Volume

Instead of amplifying everything, Novaural identifies exactly which speech frequencies you struggle with — and adjusts only those, leaving healthy frequencies untouched.

Our technology evaluates your speech sound discrimination — the ability to tell apart similar-sounding speech sounds like "thin" vs "sin" or "fan" vs "van" — at specific frequencies using a quick, scientifically validated test.

Based on your results, the system generates precise, per-frequency adjustments that can be applied to the equaliser of any compatible device. Only the frequencies where you show a measured deficit are modified — typically by just 1–3 dB.

The result? Speech becomes clearer without any overall volume increase. Your brain no longer needs to strain to decode what it's hearing, and your healthy frequencies remain protected at their natural levels.

Objective, Not Subjective

Unlike generic "sound profiles" or EQ presets based on preference, Novaural's adjustments are derived from a psychophysically validated speech discrimination test — measuring actual perception, not opinion. Two listeners with identical hearing tests may need different adjustments based on how their brain processes speech, and our technology captures this.

🌍
